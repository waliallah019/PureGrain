What is a Letter of Credit?

A Letter of Credit is a formal financial instrument issued by your bank, guaranteeing that payment will be made to us once all agreed shipment and documentation conditions are fulfilled. It is not merely a payment method — it is a legally binding commitment backed by your banking institution, ensuring complete protection for both the buyer and the seller. For high-value leather hide shipments crossing international borders, it is the most trusted, most used, and most secure trade finance instrument in the world.

We use LC not to complicate the process — but to protect your investment and ours at every stage of the shipment.

Our LC Process — Step by Step

From your first inquiry to leather hides delivered at your door — every step made clear.

  1. 1

    Submit Your Inquiry

    Send us your hide requirements — type, grade, quantity, and destination. We respond within 24–48 business hours with a detailed proforma invoice covering all specifications and pricing.

  2. 2

    Review & Sign Sales Agreement

    Once pricing and terms are agreed, we issue a formal Sales Contract outlining product specifications, quantity, Incoterms, lead time, and all LC requirements.

  3. 3

    Your Bank Issues the LC

    You instruct your bank to issue an irrevocable Documentary Letter of Credit in our favour, strictly in line with the terms of the signed Sales Contract.

  4. 4

    We Review & Accept the LC

    Our bank reviews the LC upon receipt. If all terms comply with the contract, we formally accept and begin production and shipment preparation.

  5. 5

    Production, Grading & Quality Inspection

    Your leather hides are manufactured or selected to agreed specifications. Every batch undergoes grading and quality inspection before packaging and dispatch.

  6. 6

    Shipment & Document Preparation

    Hides are shipped per agreed Incoterms. We prepare a complete document set: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin, Quality Inspection Certificate, and any REACH compliance documents required.

  7. 7

    Documents Presented — Payment Released

    Documents are presented to our bank and forwarded to yours. Upon verification of full compliance, payment is released and your shipment is cleared for delivery.
